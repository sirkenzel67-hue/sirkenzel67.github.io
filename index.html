EVs and ICE Cars Comparison

This research project provides a comparative analysis of Electronic Vehicles (EVs) and Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles, focusing on three primary pillars: Capital Expenditure (CapEx), Operational Expenditure (OpEx) per kilometer, and Performance Dynamics.
